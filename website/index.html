What we do?

Ditsa KiranaKart is solving some major problems for the rural retail market and also acting as a platform to generate employment for the rural people. Our startup provides market access; by providing a platform as a marketplace for the manufactured products of DWCRA (Group's Development of Women and Children in Rural Area), we are helping to solve the problem of market access.
